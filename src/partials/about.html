About the Studio

Who We Are

Luna Nune Interactive is an independent studio founded in 2021 by designers, narrative developers, and UI thinkers passionate about transforming the match-3 genre.

Our goals are simple:
• Make games that breathe and think.
• Replace stress with resonance.
• Design for depth and elegance, not just dopamine.

Our team is remote-first, with contributors across Europe, Asia, and South America — all bound by the same mission:
To craft puzzle experiences that feel poetic, precise, and playable for years.
